Well, onto the subject of kittens. I love the idea of kittens, and truly wish I could have some.

A few examples of why I would love to have some kittens include, in order of importance:

  1. My wife loves them
  2. I love my wife
  3. They are snuggly
  4. They are tiny

Unfortunately. I'm allergic.

Things that suck about this include, in no particular order:

  • My wife misses out
  • We don't have any ferocious kittens to protect our home
  • There is no nascent hunter in our home to bring prey home to us stoopid hoomans
  • There is nobody to arbitrarily (even capriciously) alternate between needing our attention, being oblivious to our existence, being annoyed at said existence, and being furious at said existence
